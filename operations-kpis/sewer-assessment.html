Monthly Wastewater Gravity Main Assessment
Calculation Note

The performance of this KPI is measured based on the total footage of wastewater gravity mains inspected by CCTV during the fiscal year compared to the total system length.

Total system length: 5,200 miles

Target: At least 10% of the total system (~520 miles) inspected annually.

How: A remote-controlled camera visually inspects every foot of the inside of underground sewer pipes (CCTV inspection).
