Calculation Note
Hours: SSOEndDateTime minus response start, floored at 0.
Start: SRInitDateTime when valid; otherwise SSOStartDateTime or IncidentDatetime.
End: always SSOEndDateTime.

Response Hours = (SSOEndDateTime − StartForResponse) × 24, minimum 0.

StartForResponse priority: if SR exists → use SRInitDateTime (unless SRInitDateTime > SSOEndDateTime, then fall back to SSOStartDateTime or IncidentDatetime); if no SR → use SSOStartDateTime or IncidentDatetime.

SSOEndDateTime is always used as the end time.
